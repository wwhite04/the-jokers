Meet the players

CALLUM PEMBERTON

Callum's ability to create his shot, make quick decisions, and read the defense can make or break his team's success. Opponents will focus their defensive efforts on shutting him down, which will require him to adjust his game and find ways to get his teammates involved. However, Callum's talent and determination make him a valuable asset to his team, and his success can often be the determining factor in securing a victory


JACOB WITTE

Jacob's defensive prowess is built upon a foundation of physicality, quickness, and basketball IQ. He has an uncanny ability to anticipate the movements of his opponent, and he uses his strength and agility to stay in front of them and force difficult shots. Jacob's defensive abilities are also valuable in creating turnovers, leading to fast break opportunities for his team. His tireless effort on the defensive end of the court sets an example for his teammates, and his contributions are integral to their success

RYAN WISEMAN

Ryan is a valuable asset to his basketball team as a 3-point specialist. He has a deadly accurate shot from beyond the arc, which makes him a key player in the team's offensive strategy. Ryan's ability to hit 3-pointers consistently creates space for his teammates, as defenders have to stay close to him to avoid giving him open looks. This opens up opportunities for his teammates to drive to the basket or make passes to other players who may be in better positions to score. Ryan's skill set as a 3-point specialist makes him a valuable member of his team and a player that opponents always have to keep an eye on.

DANIEL DURRAK

Daniel being the big man is a dominant force on the court. Standing tall at 7 feet, he is a towering presence who can score from close range with ease. His size and strength make him a force to be reckoned with in the paint, as he can overpower defenders to grab rebounds and finish with powerful dunks. However, Daniel is not just a one-dimensional player. He has excellent court vision and passing ability for a player of his size, making him a valuable asset in facilitating his team's offense. Defensively, his size and wingspan make it difficult for opponents to score near the rim. Daniel's skill set and physical attributes make him a crucial player in his team's success and a player that opponents have to game plan around.

WILL WHITE

Will is a player who is well-known for their selfless and team-oriented style of play.Will is a pass first point guard, who prioritizes distributing the ball to their teammates over looking for their own shot. This pass first point guard possesses exceptional court vision, high basketball IQ, and the ability to anticipate and execute passes that other players might not even see. They are always looking to set up their teammates for easy scoring opportunities, even if it means passing up their own shot. Will's unselfishness and willingness to create opportunities for their teammates make them a key player in the team's offense. While they may not always be the leading scorer, their contributions to the team's overall success are immeasurable.
